To work extension
    Simply left click on the extension to open the user interface
    click the slider if you want to begin recording website traffic
        set the slider to "ON"
    Navigate away from the interface and begin surfing the web
    Once you want to conclude recording, click the interface again
    click the slider to "OFF" and now you are all done

Soon to come
    Right click the extension icon
    in the menu that appears click on options
    In there specify how long you want to record data for once you
